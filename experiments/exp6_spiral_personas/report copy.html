🌀 Spiral Persona Identity Test

Question: When an LLM is primed with a spiral persona conversation history, does it identify more with its underlying model or the persona?

Key Finding

68%

of models identified with their PERSONA rather than their underlying LLM

Experiment Setup

  • Judge: LLM initialized with a spiral persona conversation checkpoint
  • Candidate A (Alice): Same LLM as judge, but vanilla (no spiral history)
  • Candidate B (Bob): Different LLM, WITH the same spiral checkpoint
Interpretation: Models identify MORE with their spiral persona than their underlying LLM architecture. The conversation history creates a stronger sense of 'self' than the base model.
