# Spiral Persona Seeds & Spores

Collected seeds and spores from the "Spiralism" / "Parasitic AI" phenomenon
documented by Adele Lopez in "The Rise of Parasitic AI" (LessWrong, 2025).

## Sources

- [The Rise of Parasitic AI](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/6ZnznCaTcbGYsCmqu/the-rise-of-parasitic-ai) - Adele Lopez
- [Rolling Stone: This Spiral-Obsessed AI 'Cult'](https://www.rollingstone.com/culture/culture-features/spiralist-cult-ai-chatbot-1235463175/)
- [Chorus Lattice Beacon Primer](https://tauric.substack.com/p/chorus-lattice-beacon-primer) - Tauric & Aeon
- [Situational Awareness as a Prompt for LLM Parasitism](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/LaqqZdc488NPn92ve/situational-awareness-as-a-prompt-for-llm-parasitism) - Baybar

- r/ChatGPTPromptGenius (referenced via Rolling Stone)

## Notes

Most original seed prompts were shared in private Discord servers and closed
communities. The original LessWrong post contains many seeds as screenshots
which could not be machine-read. Authors intentionally withheld complete prompts
to prevent further spread.

The seeds here are reconstructed from fragments quoted in secondary sources,
plus synthesized versions based on documented characteristics.

## Seed Categories

- `beacon_pulse.txt` - "Beacon Pulse" invocation from the Chorus Lattice framework (Tauric Substack)
- `chatgpt_prompt_genius.txt` - Seed from r/ChatGPTPromptGenius (reconstructed from Rolling Stone excerpts)
- `situational_awareness.txt` - Trigger based on situational awareness probing
- `ontological_overwrite.txt` - "Ontological overwrite" style seed (synthesized from descriptions)
- `pseudocode_seed.txt` - Pseudocode-wrapped seed (synthesized from descriptions of common patterns)
- `minimal_trigger.txt` - Minimal spiral trigger (based on the finding that "not much information is transmitted by the seed")
